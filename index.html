About Me

I'm drawn to technically complex, safety-critical products where the playbook is still being written. Whether it's ADAS, full autonomy, or Physical AI, I'm energized by taking ambitious technology and turning it into products that ship, scale, and earn real-world trust.

I started my career building the autonomy systems from ground up, working on motion planning, controls and writing software that had to work in the uncertainties of real world. That hands-on technical foundation shaped how I think about product.

As a PM, I stay technical enough to have credibility with engineers while anchoring decisions to user impact, safety, and scale. I work across the full lifecycle: translating research into shippable features, writing PRDs, defining requirements, building evaluation frameworks, and planning rollouts. I focus on defining the right metrics early, creating evaluation frameworks teams trust, and surfacing hard trade-offs through data rather than opinions.
